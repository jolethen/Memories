The Beginning

It all started when hosin messaged me Hi jole, ive made a multicraft server and im looking for helpers do you want to be ranked in it? I was like sure, the date was 23 April, then at 24 April i made the discord server for square blox, as multibug had a month worth of time before u can actually announce your server globally so we worked our all ass on, for the server, so that we can get some players. Our grind began but this part was just filled with alot of *HOPE* thinking if we do our best possible we can we will get gud players. And exactly the opposite happened we struggled to get even a single player, then we met with some of our long time players like Toasted, senpai_spider, Milan(The Silent legend). Milan never spoke but his presence mad eit worth while, then milan jist suddenly disappeared and we haven't met him since.. After that we planned to host a world cup inside our server, we were in the early phase of server soo, we thought maybe then we can attract some players due to worldcup. Long story short, no one joined I just lost hope if doing your best can actually get u some grindy players. Well, from my experience with Eternal Elements a server my friend himesandra ran i already knew that sometimes you dont just need that hard work, a chunk of luck is also what you need. If you hit that milestone in the start of your server its crazy and harmful at the same time, If your server picks up godspeed pace upon adding just a new mod or restarting a new server, Its just like the needle in hail once u get that pace you'll be stressed and both greedy that "I can fo anything and anything i do will succeed there isnt a chance of it failing" This is the biggest mis conception I've saw this with 5 servers now, I wont name them or it's like disrespecting them (one of which belonged to me xD). Long before Square blox, Eternal elements, Techblox i just had a dream to make a MMORPG server i even started the work on it just after i joined multicraft, that server failed horribly i didn't even got a single player to play, my hardwork of more then 2 months just got ruined. I was just like crazy after this happened, in my early days of minetest i used to play a server known as "Survival Go" I barely spent 5-6 months on that server but thats the server where i got introduced to whats true multiplayer gaming, after that it got discontinued and then the season 2 of survival go(sugo) started i was really happy that the world i once played is back but upon joining i saw that the map have resetted at that time i wasn't on discord, so i didn't got any information on the reset or stuff, then jist after a week or two sugo felt like nothing but yet another normal minetest server, Thenn, one of the most important thing happened to my life which was the introduction to Adventure Time, at that time i didn't knew what was adventure time actually all i was told it was a gud server with pvp and wars with other people. That was the biggest shift in my life, shifting from a peaceful survival world of sugo to AdvTime then i met one of my biggest enemy in AT who was Alexander_The_Great, bud actually felt like a 5 star villan at first and till the very last of AT, i got helper rank in AT then suddenly stuff changed, people started to leave AT and i also felt like crumbled, no one to talk to, no one to fight and especially no one to even care that i was there or not. Now this was the time i was introduced to Welcome to South a small multicraft server then Ectroncraft another multicraft server, and finally Eternal Elements which just like changed my pov of what i can actually do and make out of an empty space, I had fun even when there was no one to play woth, no one to talk with but just that satisfaction of building something even when not recognised
